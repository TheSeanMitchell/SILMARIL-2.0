# Quadrant Leaderboards & Champion Election — Now Visible

The ARENA tab now shows all four quadrants' independent leaderboards and champion elections:

**CRYPTO leaderboard** (updated live)
- Shows top 8 strategies for the crypto universe
- Elected champion highlighted and shown at the top
- Edge per trade and win rate for each strategy

**STOCK leaderboard** (independent)
- Top 8 strategies tested on the stock universe
- Stock's own champion election (different from crypto's)

**METALS leaderboard** (awaiting data)
- Top 8 strategies for metals once data starts flowing
- Metal's own champion once strategies accumulate trades

**ENERGY leaderboard** (awaiting data)
- Top 8 strategies for energy once daily feed populates
- Energy's own champion independent of the other three

Each quadrant shows:
- Its elected champion (bold, highlighted)
- Why it won (the election reason from `champion_<quadrant>.json`)
- The full leaderboard of strategies tested on that market, ranked by edge
- Trades, edge per trade, and win rate for each strategy

This is where you'll see the regime playoff system take shape: watch which strategies dominate each quadrant on different days as volatility and regime shifts.
